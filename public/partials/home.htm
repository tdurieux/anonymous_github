Usage

  1. Login with Github access your dashboard and anonymize your repositories.

Example of an anonymized repository: https://anonymous.4open.science/r/840c8c57-3c32-451e-bf12-0e20be300389/.

Double-anonymous Anonymize your repositories.

Anonymous Github allows you to simply anonymize your Github repository. Several anonymization options are available to ensure that you do not break the double-anonymize such as removing links, images or specific terms. You still keep control of your repository, define an expiration date to make your repository unavailable after the review.

Explorer Navigate the content.

The reviewers can explore your repository with ease, the source code is highlighted, PDFs, images, Notebook are rendered. The goal is to make is as easy as possible for the reviewer to explore and review the repository. GitHub Pages is also supported, but not static site generators, such as Jekyll.

Manage Keep an eyes on your repositories.

Keep a view in your anonymized repositories. Edit your anonymization configuration, remove your repository and update the content of your repository. You can also monitor the access and the number of views of your repository.
